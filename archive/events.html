Events

The Centre for Planetary Sciences at the University of Toronto provides opportunities to engage with top international researchers.

Lunches @ Scarborough

Every Wednesday from 11am-12pm we meet in the SW506 conference room for an informal discussion of current topics in planetary science over lunch. This is an opportunity for CPS members from across the three campuses to get together on a regular basis and allow cross-pollination of ideas.

If you would like to receive reminder emails and announcements of the topic for the week, please email us.


Planet Days

The CPS organizes and hosts 6 planet Days each year at the University of Toronto, Scarborough campus. Each retreat typically involves 2 distinguished international visitors and 1 UofT speaker. Ample time is reserved for discussions and new exchanges of ideas. These retreats serve as gathering points for the entire University of Toronto Earth / Solar System / Exoplanet community.
