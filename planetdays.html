A core activity of the CPS takes the form of planet days, hosted on the University of Toronto's Scarborough campus, in the spirit of day-long academic retreats. During each event, invited and local speakers deliver lectures on selected topics in both planetary and exoplanet science. Ample time is reserved for questions and comments after the lectures in order to promote discussions of current issues.
