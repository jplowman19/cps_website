Educational Activities

The Centre for Planetary Sciences at the University of Toronto provides fellowships for undergraduate and graduate research.

The CPS typically offers two undergraduate and two graduate research fellowships each year. All UofT students working on a topic in planetary/exoplanet science are invited to apply.

CPS Graduate Fellowships

The Centre for Planetary Sciences offers fellowship support for graduate students working on research in planetary sciences. Each fellowship amounts to $5000+$5000 spread over two years, which can be used to cover research expenses such as travel, computer, books, etc. Typically, nominations are submitted by graduate departments to promote their best graduate student applicants each year.

CPS Summer Undergraduate Fellowships

The Centre for Planetary Sciences offers fellowship support for undergraduate students carrying out summer research in planetary sciences. Several fellowships valued at $4000-$4500 each are available each year. To apply, please submit a CV, an official transcript and 1-page research plan (prepared in coordination with your faculty advisor) to CPS director Kristen Menou via email: kristen.menou@utoronto.ca. The deadline for these applications is March 15 each year.
